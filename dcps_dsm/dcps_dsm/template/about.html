Mission & Vision

We believe psychology isn't just a subject in a textbook — it's a tool for understanding and solving everyday problems. Every initiative we run is guided by that belief.

MISSION

Our Mission

To raise mental health awareness among students through research, training, and community-driven initiatives — and to build a supportive community around the practical application of psychology.

VISION

Our Vision

We envision a Bangladesh where talking about mental health is normal, not stigmatized — and where every student has access to the right support when they need it.

Our Journey

Since its founding, DCPS has grown step by step — from a small student initiative to a nationally recognised organisation.

2024

Founding

Dhaka College Psychology Society was founded with the goal of spreading psychological literacy and mental health awareness among students.

2024 – 2025

First Research & Training Initiatives

Focus Group Discussions, scale-development research, and the first round of seminars and workshops were organised, reaching 150+ participants.

2025

Best Youth Performing Partner Award

DCPS received this award in recognition of its leadership on the "Peaceweavers" national project, delivered in partnership with Psycoach BD and BRAC.

2025

Awareness Campaign Across 3 Campuses

A 7-day Mental Health Awareness Campaign was carried out across Dhaka College, Eden Mohila College, and Begum Badrunnesa Government Girls' College.

2026

First Mental Health Assessment Campaign

Dhaka College hosted its first full-scale Mental Health Assessment Campaign.
