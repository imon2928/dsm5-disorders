About Us

The Dhaka College Psychology Society (DCPS) is a student-led organisation within the Psychology Department at Dhaka College. We bring psychology beyond the classroom through research, awareness, and skill-building. Our Skill-Based Psychology Hub publishes student writing, research, and learning resources across six thematic lobes.
