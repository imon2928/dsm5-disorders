Asked Questions

What is needed to be a member of DCPS?+
Students from any department of Dhaka College can become members. Students do not need to be from the Psychology Department; anyone with an interest in psychology can apply.
How to contact for research?+
Select **"Research Collaboration"** as the subject in the form above and submit a brief description of your proposal. Our research team will contact you.
I would like to invite you for a seminar or workshop. How can I do that? ?+
Select **"Seminar / Workshop Invitation"** as the subject and mention the date, topic, and expected number of participants.
